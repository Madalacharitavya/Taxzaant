Mission&Vision

        The mission statement of a tax office usually focuses on the core purpose of the agency.

        Collection of Revenue: Ensuring the timely and accurate collection of taxes to fund 
        public goods and services.
        Fairness and Equity: Applying tax laws uniformly and fairly across all taxpayer.
         ensuring compliance while protecting taxpayer rights.
        Compliance and Enforcement: Promoting voluntary compliance with tax laws while preventing tax
         evasion and enforcing penalties when necessary.
        Customer Service: Providing taxpayers with the support, information, and resources they need
         to comply with tax obligations.
    
            The vision statement reflects the long-term goals and aspirations of the tax office:
    
            Modernization and Innovation: Leveraging technology and data analytics to improve tax 
            administration, reduce fraud, and streamline taxpayer interactions.
            Transparency and Accountability: Building public trust through transparency in operations,
             clear communication, and accountability for the office’s actions.
            Economic Growth and Stability: Supporting the nation’s economic development by ensuring
             a stable and predictable revenue base.
            Simplified Processes: Making tax compliance simpler and more accessible for individuals
             and businesses through automation and user-friendly systems.
